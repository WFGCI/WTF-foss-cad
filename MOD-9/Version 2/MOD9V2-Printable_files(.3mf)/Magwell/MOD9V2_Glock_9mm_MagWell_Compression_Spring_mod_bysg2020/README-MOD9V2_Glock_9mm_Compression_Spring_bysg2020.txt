
Instructions for magwell and mag catch
Use a m3 x 4mm long heat insert OR 6-32 x .15" long heat insert 
drill with a 3/16" drill bit
M3 x 14 screw OR 6-32" x .5" 
the screw should thread in past the heat insert and into the mag catch which will act like a nylock to secure the screw. 
Spring:
.18" OD x .025" WG cut to .5 inch
