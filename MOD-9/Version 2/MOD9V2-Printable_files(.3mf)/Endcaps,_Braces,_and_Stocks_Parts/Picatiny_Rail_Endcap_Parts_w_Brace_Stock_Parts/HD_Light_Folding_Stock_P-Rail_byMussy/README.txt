Summary

This is an HD Folding Stock that fits the new MOD22x new P-rail endcap and is compatible with most P-rail stock adaptors out there, this is a HEAVY DUTY and comfortable design, more stocks coming very soon ;)
Parts Required:2 x M3 screws for the butt pad, M4 for the hinge & M3 countersunk for the lock pin
As usual all building details will be in the make. If you are interested in other firearm parts and designs do join our group on Matrix: Gun_Cad_Ideas the coolest prop team ever
